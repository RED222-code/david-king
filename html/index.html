About

I’m a full-stack developer with five years of experience building web applications across the stack. My work covers freelance client projects and internal engineering tools, including dashboards, AI-powered integrations, restaurant apps, and client management portals.

I focus on responsive React frontends, secure Node.js APIs, and well-structured MongoDB or Firebase backends. The goal in every project is the same: deliver software that is clean, maintainable, and fast to use on both mobile and desktop.
